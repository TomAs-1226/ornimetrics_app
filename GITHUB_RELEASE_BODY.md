## What's Included

This is the **first public release** of Ornimetrics! Here's everything included:

### Core Features
- **Real-Time Bird Detection** - Live feed from smart bird feeders with automatic species identification
- **Species Analytics** - Beautiful pie charts, trends, and statistics for detected species
- **Photo Gallery** - Browse all captured feeder images with species tags
- **Detection History** - Complete log of all bird visits with timestamps

### AI & Identification
- **AI Species Identifier** - Take a photo and let AI identify the bird species
- **ChatGPT Integration** - Ask questions about birds and get AI-powered answers
- **Confidence Scores** - See how certain the AI is about each identification
- **Scientific Names** - Lookup and display scientific nomenclature

### Field Observations
- **Manual Logging** - Record bird sightings with full metadata
- **My Field Detections** - View all your saved observations with photos
- **Location Tracking** - GPS coordinates automatically attached
- **Weather Integration** - Current conditions logged with each observation
- **Per-User Storage** - Your observations stay private to your account

### Tools & Utilities
- **Birdwatching Session Timer** - Dynamic Island-style floating timer
- **Data Export** - CSV, JSON, and text report exports
- **Offline Mode** - Access cached data without internet
- **Home Screen Widget** - Quick stats at a glance

### Social & Community
- **Community Center** - Share posts and sightings with fellow birders
- **User Profiles** - Create your birding identity
- **Like & Comment** - Interact with community posts

### Customization
- **Light/Dark Themes** - Choose your preferred appearance
- **Custom Accent Colors** - Personalize with your favorite color
- **Notification Controls** - Configure alerts and reminders
- **Haptic Feedback** - Toggle tactile responses

## System Requirements

**Android:**
- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher
- ~50 MB storage space
- Internet connection for cloud features
- Optional: GPS, Camera

---

## Installation

1. Download the appropriate APK from the assets below
2. Open the downloaded file on your Android device
3. Enable "Install from unknown sources" if prompted
4. Tap Install and wait for completion
5. Open Ornimetrics and sign in or create an account
